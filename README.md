This project analyzes electronic product sales across five Australian cities using an interactive dashboard built in Power BI. The goal of the project was to visualize revenue performance, identify top-selling products, and track sales trends over time.
The dashboard highlights key metrics such as total revenue and total units sold. It also compares revenue by product category and by city to understand which markets and products contribute the most to overall sales.
A time-series chart was included to analyze revenue trends across months, helping identify periods of decline and recovery. Interactive filters allow users to explore sales performance by selecting specific cities or product categories.
Through this analysis, the dashboard reveals that phones generate the highest revenue among product categories, while certain cities contribute more significantly to total sales. This project demonstrates the use of Power BI for transforming raw data into meaningful business insights.
